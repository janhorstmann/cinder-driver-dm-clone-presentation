# Motivation

local storage:
* low-cost
  * e.g.: small-scale, edge cloud, ...
* little operational knowledge
  * SDS can be complex
* low latency
  * high IO, but no HA

in OpenStack:
* nova root/ephemeral storage
  * bound to instance lifecycle
* PCI-passthrough of NVMe-Drives
  * bound to host
